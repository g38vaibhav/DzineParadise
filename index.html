VAASTU

Welcome to Dzine Paradise, where we seamlessly blend modern interior design concepts with the ancient principles of Vaastu Shastra to create harmonious and balanced living spaces. We understand the significance of Vastu in creating homes that not only look beautiful but also radiate positive energy and wellbeing.

Vaastu Shastra, an ancient Indian architectural science, focuses on the layout and design of living spaces to align them with the natural forces and elements. It encompasses principles that consider the orientation, placement, and arrangement of various elements in a building, such as the entrance, rooms, kitchen, and more, to ensure a harmonious environment for the inhabitants.
